## Ranking Methodology

**Opportunity Score (0–100)** measures an account's capacity to fuel your own content creation through:

- **Original framing** – creates new angles, not just rehashing news
- **Early signal** – posts before breaking developments hit mainstream
- **Quote-tweet fuel** – posts are provocative enough to reply to or build upon
- **Conversation duration** – threads and discussions that last hours or days
- **Write-worthiness** – frequently creates content worth analyzing, rebutting, or expanding
- **Agenda-setting** – consistently leads rather than follows the conversation
