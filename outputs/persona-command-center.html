Hybrid gathering strategy

  • Persona keyword searches Query X with persona-specific terms, exclusions, and freshness windows.
  • Curated account/list monitoring Watch accounts that reliably surface domain-specific conversation early.
  • X trends by region/category Use broader trends as context, not as the primary source of truth.
  • News/RSS fallback Corroborate topics before drafting, especially for high-risk or fast-moving claims.
  • Velocity comparison Compare each scan with prior snapshots to separate real movement from ambient chatter.
